About This Game

Imagine a 3D multiplayer go-kart bonanza where the track isn't just for racing, but for full-on vehicular mayhem! Welcome to Smash Karts, where elegance meets anarchy, and every turn could lead to either a victory parade or a spectacular kart-smashing spectacle.

Smash Karts is a online driving battle game, as you hit the start button, you're zooming through a kaleidoscope of maps, from candyland nightmares to neon-lit cityscapes. But it's not just about who crosses the finish line first; it's about who remains un-smashed! You'll encounter a barrage of weaponry from your fellow kart jockeys—guns, missiles, mines, and yes, even nukes. Your mission? Stay alive, smash others, and rack up those sweet, sweet XP points. Just keep an eye out for those gift boxes; they're your lifeline for arming up with random weapons.

Controls

  • Up Arrow or W: Gas
  • Down Arrow or S: Brake
  • Left Arrow or A: Steer Left
  • Right Arrow or D: Steer Right
  • Space: Fire

How to Play Smash Karts

  • Head over to OnlineGames.io, find Smash Karts, and dive in by clicking that big, shiny "Play" button.
  • Use your arrow keys or WASD to steer, but remember, in this game, driving straight might be the quickest way to your kart's funeral.
  • Collect those question-marked boxes for a surprise weapon. You only carry one at a time, so choose your moments wisely. Got a mine? Drop it where the traffic's thickest. Gun or nuke? Aim true and obliterate the competition.
  • Each box you drive through is like a Christmas present from a slightly unhinged Santa. Mines, nukes, guns—oh my! Use them to climb that leaderboard.
  • If you're the last kart standing, you win! It's like musical chairs, but with more explosions.
  • Earn coins and gems to deck out your kart with the craziest skins and characters. Fancy a clown driving a disco ball kart? Here's your chance.

Game Modes Explained

There are three game modes you can choose in custom multiplayer rooms. Apart from modes you can enable teams, set round duration between 3 to 20 mins, customize weapons and more.

  • Free For All (FFA): It's every kart for themselves! Collect weapons from those mysterious boxes and use them to smash opponents. The player with the most kills at the end of the round wins. It's pure, unadulterated kart chaos.
  • Gem Collector: Scattered around the arena are gems. Your mission is to collect more gems than your rivals before time runs out. Think of it like a treasure hunt, but with a lot more explosions.
  • Hat Holder: One hat to rule them all! Find and hold onto the hat for as long as you can while everyone else tries to take it from you. You can't use weapons while carrying the hat, so it's all about evasion and cunning.
