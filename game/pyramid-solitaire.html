About This Game

Pyramid Solitaire is a free online card game at OnlineGames.io. In this calming card puzzle, you need to remove a pyramid of cards by selecting pairs that add up to 13. It's simple but fun to learn. Choose a deck, clear your mind, and see how quickly you can remove the pyramid. Play Pyramid Solitaire now!

Controls

  • Tap (or click) on a card to select it.
  • Click another card that totals 13 to clear the pair.
  • Click the deck at the bottom to draw a card when stuck.

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

Begin at the top layers (cards at the bottom). Only uncovered cards can be matched. Match pairs to 13.

Kings are 13 by themselves, Queens are 12, Jacks are 11, and all number cards are at face value. You can match cards of any color. For example, 5♥ and 7♣.

Possible Combinations

  • King (13)
  • Queen (12) + Ace (1)
  • Jack (11) + 2
  • 10 + 3
  • 9 + 4
  • 8 + 5
  • 7 + 6

Play strategically to uncover all pyramid cards. If no matches, click the deck to draw. You can go through the deck multiple times. Clear as many pyramid cards as possible. When choosing between matches, pick the one that exposes more covered cards.
