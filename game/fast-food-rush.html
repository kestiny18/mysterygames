Fast Food Rush

About This Game

You're at Burger King, sinking your teeth into a juicy cheeseburger when suddenly - what if YOU ran the show? Fast Food Rush lets you experience the thrilling chaos of managing your own burger empire! Start with just 2,500 coins and grow across 3 locations - will you become the ultimate fast food tycoon?

🔥 Sizzling Gameplay

  • Build from a single grill to a multi-branch empire
  • Upgrade equipment, seating, and staff efficiency
  • Expand into coffee and donut shops for maximum profits

🎮 Simple Controls

PC: WASD/Arrows to move • Scroll to zoom

Mobile: Tap & drag to move

💰 Pro Tips

  • Keep tables clean for happy customers
  • Hire and train staff to automate processes
  • Balance upgrades across all locations
