About This Game

In a post-apocalyptic 2D world, you awaken with nothing in your pockets but the will to survive in your heart. Wander around the arena for anything that might help you survive in a place swarming with enemies. Found a bottle of cola? Toss it in your backpack. Did you see a vest and helmet? Gear up for protection. Come across an MP40 or a box of grenades? Consider yourself lucky; you're making progress!

Suroi io is an online multiplayer survival game inspired by Surviv.io. Your mission: gather loot, chests, weapons, medkits, and whatever else you can find to survive in this hostile environment. Explore, avoid enemies, and that toxic orange gas. Collect your essentials and eliminate opponents. Soon, they'll be the ones fleeing from you. Dominate the field and carve your name on the leaderboard.

Controls

  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Aim: Mouse
  • Attack: Left-Click
  • Change Weapons: 1,2,3 or Mouse Scroll
  • Pickup/Interact: F
  • Reload: R
  • Use Healing Item: Left-Click on the item or 7-0
  • Cancel Action: X
  • Toggle Fullscreen Map: G or M
  • Toggle Minimap: N
  • Use Emote Wheel: Hold Right-Click, drag, and release
  • Team Ping: Hold Right-Click and C, drag, and release
  • Request Item: Hold C and click on the item
  • Drop Item: Right-click on the item
  • Equip Last Item: Q
  • Swap Gun Slots: T
  • Equip Other Gun: Space
  • Change Scope Zoom: Left-Click on Scope

How to Play

  • Suroi is a battle royale io game where strategic thinking is key to survival.
  • First, collect armor, weapons, medikits, and ammo.
  • To find them, enter abandoned houses, break into closets, or open boxes by left-clicking—press F to equip your findings.
  • Once you're armed enough, defend your team and take down opponents. Dodge enemy attacks and send them to spectator mode to watch how you've got there!

Healing Items

In duo mode, you can revive your teammate, and they can revive you. In solo mode, you're on your own, so stock up on healing items. Save especially the Medikits for an emergency when you need it. Here's what you need to know to stay in the fight:

  • Gauze: Restores 15 health
  • Medikit: Restores 100 health
  • Cola: Restores 25 adrenaline
  • Tablets: Restore 50 adrenaline

Beware the Orange Gas

  • Enemies aren't your only threat. In this post-apocalyptic world, a toxic orange gas spreads across the map, killing anyone who breathes it in. Keep an eye on the map in the top-left corner of your screen. When you see the orange shadow, run.
  • As the game progresses, the gas will cover more of the map, shrinking the safe zone.
  • Now you're ready to survive in Suroi.io! May your health bar stay full, and good luck out there!
