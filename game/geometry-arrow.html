About This Game

When you hit start, you don't just fire the arrow; you become itself. Race through a dark, hidden tunnel in Geometry Arrow. It's a super sleek, rhythm-driven platformer game like Geometry Dash online that puts your reflexes to the test. You guide a simple white arrow through a maze of sharp angles and spikes. You'll be rising and falling with a single tap. Across 12 difficult levels plus a hidden bonus stage, each set to its own upbeat music, you'll race against geometry itself. With clean, minimal graphics and instantly graspable controls, Geometry Arrow is a free browser game you can play online whenever you need a quick thrill on the go. Just a friendly reminder: take a breather when the rage becomes too intense. Trust me, you'll need it.

Controls

  • PC: Left mouse button or Spacebar: hold to ascend, release to descend
  • Mobile: Touch anywhere on the screen: press to lift, let go to drop

How to Play Geometry Arrow

  • From the moment your arrow appears at the left edge, it's all about timing. Press and hold (or tap and hold) to gently rise. Release when you need to fall.
  • Early levels ease you in, introducing one hazard at a time, like simple spikes, sawblades, and split ramps.
  • You'll learn that a quick tap clears a small spike while a longer hold soars over tall spikes.
  • Pay attention to the beat: each level's music isn't just background noise. A rising drumroll often hints at a tall barrier ahead, and a sudden bass drop might mean you need to let go and dive under a low overhang.
  • As you progress, obstacles compound. You'll navigate narrow corridors of spikes by tapping so fast your arrow darts in rapid zig-zags to slip past.
  • You'll see your progress in the bar at the top. Don't trust if you're near the end because of one little mistake, and you're back at the beginning. No checkpoints.

Developer

Geometry Arrow is distributed by Playgama. It's perfect for fans of quick-play Geometry Dash games.
