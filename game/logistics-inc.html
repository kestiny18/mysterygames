Logistics Inc

About This Game

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that, right in your browser. This addictive online idle game starts you with five employees, $10, and a single bicycle courier. Build your fleet, manage staff, research upgrades, and dominate markets in this deep business simulator that's perfect for quick sessions or marathon strategy planning.

Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Click or tap to play

How to Play

Start with one bicycle - buy it, assign staff, and watch orders roll in. Each completed delivery fills your status bar and bank account. Empty vehicles don't earn - always keep them staffed!

Expand Your Empire

Reinvest profits to unlock vans, trucks, planes, and ships. Higher tiers mean bigger orders but require more workers and cash. Balance expansion with hiring!

Research & Development

Assign workers to R&D to unlock game-changing upgrades like Efficiency boosts and Contractor hiring. Early investment pays exponential dividends!

Marketing Mastery

Keep orders flowing by assigning marketing staff to different vehicle types. Balance your team across product lines for steady income.

Maintenance Matters

Hire and train mechanics to keep vehicles at peak performance. Upskill them to handle advanced fleets as you expand.

Become a Tycoon

Use bulk purchases to scale rapidly. Optimize every department until your money counter spins endlessly. How many zeros can you add to your valuation?
