About This Game

Golf Orbit is an online golf game where one powerful shot can send your ball flying across the course and even into outer space! With just a single tap, you can launch the ball as far as possible, collect coins, and upgrade your golfer's abilities. The goal is to hit the ball farther with each shot. Just try not to land in a bunker, smack into a tree, take a surprise bath in the water... or, you know, get snatched by an eagle or abducted by a UFO. Weird things may happen in Golf Orbit. ⛳

Controls

On Computers

  • Click or tap when the arrow is in the green zone for a perfect shot.
  • If the ball stops near the hole, hold the left click to aim and release to putt.

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your goal in Golf Orbit is to launch the ball as far as possible and, if you're lucky (or just really good), sink it into the hole.

When you start a round, a swing meter appears, moving back and forth like a metronome. There's a green zone in the middle, this is where you want to stop the meter for a perfect stroke. Tap at the right moment, and your golfer will send the ball soaring.

As the ball flies, it might bounce off trees, roll into bunkers, or take an unexpected dive into the water. If it stops close to the hole, you get a second shot. This time, the mechanics change: instead of a timing-based meter, you'll see an arrow indicating the ball's path. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the putt.

Score a Hole-In-One

Landing a hole-in-one is the ultimate goal in Golf Orbit, and if you pull it off, you'll be rewarded generously. If you manage to send the ball straight into the hole in a single shot, you'll score an eagle, multiplying your earnings by 10x. This means a massive coin boost, allowing you to upgrade your golfer faster and hit even farther in the next round.

However, not every shot is perfect. If your ball lands near the hole, you'll get a second chance. At this point, a more precise aiming mechanic kicks in—you'll see a guiding arrow showing the ball's path. Tap to set your shot and release at the right moment to putt the ball into the hole. If you make it on the second attempt, you'll score a birdie, which triples your earnings.

Upgrade The Stroke

Coins earned from each round can be spent on three key upgrades:

  • Strength: Boosts your golfer's power for stronger swings.
  • Speed: Makes the ball travel through the air faster.
  • Bounce: Increases how far the ball rebounds off surfaces.

Upgrading these abilities is key to hitting longer shots and setting new records. The farther you hit the ball, the more coins you collect, allowing you to improve your stats even further.

The challenge increases as you go, with more difficult landscapes and obstacles appearing. Trees, sand traps, and water hazards can slow you down or stop your progress, so learning the best angles and power levels is important. Timing your swing perfectly is the key to mastering the game.
