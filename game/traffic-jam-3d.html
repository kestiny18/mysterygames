About This Game

Are you ready to overtake vehicles at full speed on the highway? Traffic Jam 3D is a 3D driving game where you can steer your vehicle on the endless road across various modes. As you can guess, there will be heavy traffic on the road! You must drive your car well without crashing any car. Keep your eyes wide open and act like a circus monkey on this endless highway in this simulator game! Try not to scare other drivers too much.😈

Controls

Desktops and Laptops

  • Arrow Keys: Steer the Car
  • N Key: Use Nitro
  • ESC: Pause

Mobile Devices

  • Use the virtual buttons on the screen to steer the car.

Traffic Jam 3D Overview

  • Pick your car and set for a ride in this racing game! Four game modes are at your disposal: Career, Infinite, Time Against and Free.
  • You are free to experience all four of them. After selecting the game mode you want to play, you will find yourself on the highway with flowing traffic.
  • Your main objective is to fulfill the tasks given to you by navigating your car masterfully on this road.
  • While trying to do that, you cannot let any accident happen! Avoid hitting any vehicle. Otherwise, you may restart from the beginning.

Game Modes in Traffic Jam 3D

Traffic Jam 3D offers you four unique modes. Play each one and choose your favorite.

  • Career Mode: You will progress level by level. You need to reach the checkpoints in time.
  • Infinite Mode: You can choose a game map among your three options: Highway, Desert, and City. Try to go as far as you can in this mode.
  • Time Against Mode: You must cover the longest distance before your time runs out.
  • Free Mode: You can drive your car on the highway without needing to accomplish any task.

Set Your Scenario

This driving game allows you to set your own road scenario. Choose your favorite game map: Highway, Desert, or City. Decide whether you want to drive in the morning, at dusk, or at night. You can also decide whether the road will be one-way or two-way. The choice is yours!

Troubleshooting

Having trouble starting the game? Try disabling your ad blocker or opening the game in incognito mode. If the issue persists, visit our support page for further assistance.
