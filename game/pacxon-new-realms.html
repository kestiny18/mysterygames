In case you're new to world of PacXon, here's a quick Arcade 101 refresher:

What is PacXon?

PacXon (sometimes written as Pac-Xon) is a fun arcade-style puzzle game that mixes mechanics from classic games like Pac-Man and Xonix. In this fun game, you play as Pacxon, a little PacMan-like character, but instead of eating dots, your goal is to fill up the screen by building walls. As you move, Pacxon leaves a trail behind him. If you can successfully close off a section of the screen, that area becomes filled in. The goal is to fill at least 80% of the screen to complete the level. PacXon was originally a Flash game released in 2007, but 18 years later, an HTML5 version was published so it can be played online without plugins.

Controls

  • Arrow keys/ drag mouse: Move PacXon in four directions
  • Mouse swipe: For touchscreen and drag-based input

How to Play PacXon: New Realms

Your goal is to capture at least 80% of the field to move on. Yellow dots are shared open spaces, and orange dots are your territory. At the beginning of the level, all four sides are yours. You will run over open space to create a wall trail and loop back to a captured area to close the trail and claim that section. Then, it will turn into orange dots, marking your territory.

If an enemy touches you or your trail before you finish the loop, you lose a life. Keep track of your lives on the bottom left. Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top to know how close you are to finishing the level.

Types of Enemies

Each level features ghostly enemies with different movement patterns. You'll need to study their patterns and strike at the right moment to grab territory without losing lives.

  • Blue enemies: Move diagonally in captured zones.
  • Pink enemies: Move diagonally in open space.
  • Red enemies: The most aggressive: they move diagonally and destroy the walls you've built.

Grab Power-Ups

Collect sweet treats on the map for powerful boosts:

  • 🍬 (Candy): Eat enemies temporarily
  • 🍩 (Donut): Speed boost
  • 🍰 (Cake): Enemies move slower
  • 🧁 (Cupcake): Enemies freeze for a short time
