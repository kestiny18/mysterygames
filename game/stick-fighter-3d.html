About This Game

Stick Fighter 3D is a neon-style, 1v1 fighting game where two glowing stick figures battle in a side-view arena. Play solo against CPU or locally with a friend using the same keyboard. Control your stickman to punch, kick, and perform special moves to KO your opponent! Each round ends when one player's health bar is depleted. With flashy neon visuals and smooth animations, this is stickman combat like never before!

⚔️ Controls

Player 1 (Orange)

  • Move: A/D
  • Jump: W
  • Punch: F
  • Kick: G
  • Special: R

Player 2 (Blue)

  • Move: ←/→
  • Jump: ↑
  • Punch: K
  • Kick: L
  • Special: O

🎮 How to Play

  • Choose 1P (vs CPU) or 2P (vs Friend) mode
  • Deplete opponent's health using combos of punches and kicks
  • Special moves deal massive damage but require recharge
  • Score points for each round won

💥 Special Moves

Each fighter has a one-hit KO special that activates when the charge bar is full. Watch for the flash indicator! But beware - your opponent can do the same. Time it perfectly for an instant victory!
