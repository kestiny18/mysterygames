About This Game

Obby Tower Parkour Climb is a parkour game with a cool Roblox vibe. You'll climb up colorful platforms and obstacle courses to reach the top. The game has three maps named Tunnel, Tower and Stairs. The best part of this obby game is you can play alone or race with a friend in split-screen mode. Plus there are two different modes on each map: normal and challenge. Learn about them below.

Editor's Review

I've played a lot of parkour games, and Obby Tower Parkour Climb is a nice one that mixes Roblox elements with climbing challenges. It's fun, especially when you play with a friend. It's not too intense, but it keeps you coming back to beat your last climb.

Controls

Single Player Mode:

  • Move: Use W, A, S, D or the Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Press SPACE
  • Sprint: Hold L-SHIFT
  • Look Around: Move your MOUSE to look, scroll to zoom

Two Player Mode:

  • Player 1:
    • Move: W, A, S, D
    • Jump: SPACE
    • Sprint: L-SHIFT
  • Player 2:
    • Move: Arrow Keys
    • Jump: L
    • Sprint: K

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

  • First, choose if you'd like to go solo or 2 player. In Two Player Mode, you and a friend compete side by side. Each player uses different controls. The first one to reach the top wins. It's a fun way to see who can master the tower faster.
  • Next, choose a map to start: Tunnel, Tower and Stairs maps are available with increasing difficulty. We suggest you start with Tunnel if you're new to Obby games.
  • Your goal is to climb to the top of the tower. Use the controls above to move, jump, and sprint.
  • Look around by moving your mouse to plan your next moves. There's no time limit, so focus on understanding the layout and mastering your moves.
  • You can check how far you climbed on the progress bar in the top left corner of your screen.
  • If you choose the challenge mode, this adds a timer to your climb. You need to reach the top as fast as possible. Practice the routes in Normal Mode to find the quickest way up. This mode is great for testing how fast you can complete the climb without any mistakes.
