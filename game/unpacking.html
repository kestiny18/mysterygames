About This Game

Hey there, neat freaks! We're offering you the perfect mess to tidy up! Unpacking is a free online home decoration puzzle you can play right in your browser. Just moved in? Time to unpack those boxes!📦 As your mom always said: "Everything has its place". Solve dozens of unpacking puzzles to create your dream home.

How to Play

This place is a mess! We need your organizational skills. Properly arrange every item in its designated spot across 11 unique rooms.

Unpacking Basics

  • Select a room from the kitchen, bathroom, living room, bedroom, or garden
  • Drag items from the right-side panel to their proper locations
  • Correct placements will lock automatically
  • Complete rooms under 90 seconds for 3-star rewards

Advanced Organization

  • Arrange shelves and closets in Memory Mode
  • Sort drawers in Classic Mode by matching items to correctly sized boxes
  • Group duplicate items together for efficiency

Pro Tips

  • Start with large furniture first - beds before pillows!
  • Watch ads for helpful room previews when stuck
  • Think practically - TVs go across from sofas!

Controls

  • PC: Click & drag with mouse
  • Mobile: Tap & drag items
