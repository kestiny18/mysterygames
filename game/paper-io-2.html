About This Game

Welcome to the colorful world of Paper io 2! Paper io 2 is a free online game where you conquer white areas by drawing lines to establish your territory. Claim areas quickly while defending against opponents - this is the key to victory!

How to play Paper io 2?

  • 1. Enter your player name and click Play
  • 2. Draw lines to conquer territory
  • 3. Eliminate opponents by slicing their tails
  • 4. Watch player positions on the right
  • 5. Dominate the leaderboard!

Pro Tips

  • Always return to your territory or lose progress
  • Expand carefully - exposed tails are vulnerable
  • Watch for enemy colors - one hit eliminates you
  • Bonus tip: Don't eat your own tail!
