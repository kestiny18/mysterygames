About This Game

Can you hear the engine moaning? Drift Hunters Pro is a thrilling 3D game with high-quality graphics for drifting & racing fans. Play solo or compete online across 6 tracks with 10 upgradable vehicles. Earn rewards to unlock new cars and customize them to perfection!

Master the Drift

  • WASD/Arrows: Drive
  • Space: Handbrake
  • C: Change camera
  • R: Reset car
  • U: Toggle MPH/KMH
  • I: Engine on/off
  • P: Fullscreen

Ultimate Garage

Unlock 10 premium vehicles and customize every detail - from performance upgrades to visual styling. Learn pro drifting tips to maximize your rewards!
