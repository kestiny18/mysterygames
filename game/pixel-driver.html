About This Game

Sometimes, you just crave something simple. Nothing fancy. No complicated missions or stressful tasks. Just a pixel car, a colorful city, and the freedom to roam. That's it. These are exactly what Pixel Driver are all about.

In Pixel Driver, you'll hop into your pixel-style car and explore a colorful, bustling city made completely out of blocks. The city is lively with pixel police cars, fire trucks, and ambulances driving around, making the streets feel alive and busy.

This simple driving game beautifully combines old-school pixel graphics with modern 3D visuals, creating a fun and nostalgic atmosphere. You can race to beat your best time or just cruise to relax like in Slow Roads. It keeps things easy and enjoyable. So jump in your car, hit the gas, and enjoy the simplicity of driving around in this charming pixel world!

Controls

  • Drive with WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Use nitro with Shift
  • Pause or open menu with Escape

Game Modes

In Pixel Driver, you have two fun ways to play:

  • Racing Mode: Race around the pixel city as fast as you can. Your goal is to beat your best time! Watch out for other AI-controlled cars roaming around. Use nitro to speed up on straight roads, but slow down for tight corners so you don't crash.
  • Free Drive Mode: Relax and explore the city without any pressure. Drive anywhere you like, check out cool spots, or practice your driving and drifting skills. You'll still see AI cars on the streets, adding life to your cruising experience.

Developer

Pixel Driver was created by FreezeNova, famous for fun and easy-to-play online games for players of all ages.
