About This Game

Join the ultimate battle competition in the Crazy Strike Force game. Customize your character with unique style and dominate opponents. With an arsenal of deadly weapons, you'll unleash chaos on the battlefield. Take down enemies and craft your own winning strategy.

Controls

  • WASD/Arrow Keys: Move
  • Left Click: Fire
  • Right Click: Aim
  • Space: Jump
  • Q: Slide
  • C: Crouch
  • Shift: Run
  • 1/2/3: Switch Weapons
  • E/F: Interact
  • G: Throw Dynamite
  • H: Use Medkit
  • T: Chat
  • L: Toggle Cursor
  • Tab: Scoreboard
  • Esc: Pause

Armory & Skills

Choose from 20+ deadly weapons including Magnum, M4A1, AWP and more. Boost your combat with special skills like Quickdraw Ammo, UAV Ghost, and customize kill messages after eliminating enemies.

Profile & Stats

Change nicknames, track your K/D ratio, wins, headshots and other stats in your profile. Save/load game progress and upgrade your loadouts.

Earn Rewards

Watch ads to earn 5000+ gold and 1000+ diamonds, with chances to win loot boxes containing 500-2000 diamonds!

Create Rooms

Host your own battles! Set player limits, choose game modes, adjust round times, and select maps to create the perfect combat arena.
