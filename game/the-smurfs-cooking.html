About This Game

The Smurfs are back, and this time, they are in the kitchen! The Smurfs Cooking is a fun cooking game where you help Chef Smurf whip up the best cupcakes and snacks for the annual Smurf Food Festival. But he's in a bit of a smurfy pickle - he hasn't thought of a recipe yet! So it's up to you to jump behind the counter, serve hungry Smurfs, and keep the food flowing before the timer runs out.

If you love cooking games or just want to hang out with your favorite little blue friends, this sweet challenge will have you hooked. Muffins, whipped cream, juice, and cupcakes... it's all here!

Controls

  • Mouse/Touch: Tap or click to select ingredients, drag them to the plate, or serve Smurfs.

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

  • Complete levels by serving food and earning berries. Smurfs show dessert orders above their heads - muffins (plain/with toppings) or drinks.
  • Pour batter into the oven to bake muffins (don't let them burn!), then add toppings to match orders exactly.
  • Serve quickly before Smurfs get impatient! Earn berries to pass levels (e.g. 160 berries for Level 5).
  • Unlock boosters/upgrades like time extenders or auto-serve helpers as you progress.

Pro Chef Tips

  • Keep ovens busy - idle time is your enemy!
  • Prioritize impatient Smurfs with shortest patience bars.
  • Memorize topping-plate combos to work faster.
  • Watch videos for bonus rewards and spend sugar cubes wisely.

Features

  • Vibrant 3D graphics with original Smurf characters
  • Dozens of increasingly challenging levels
  • Charming cooking animations & sounds
  • Kid-friendly yet engaging gameplay
  • Multiple language support & unlockable power-ups
