About This Game

When the virus hit, cities crumbled, people vanished. The world went silent except for the groans of the undead. You're one of the few survivors of zombie apocalypse. Your only hope is a beat-up monster truck, with some spikes, plates, and just enough horsepower to push through the zombie chaos. This is your last chance to escape the city ruins. Fuel up, slam the pedal, and drive through waves of the undead in this thrilling side-scrolling zombie survival game!

Apocalypse Truck is a 2D driving game where you roam in a post apocalypse town, run over zombies and race to the finish line. Besides surviving the zombie apocalypse with wheels, spikes you will also launch off wrecked cars, perform mid air flips and and grab coins as you go. Are you into monster truck flips? Destruction? Flattening some zombies? Ok, don't wait! Start playing this fun game on our browser.

Controls

  • → Arrow keys / WASD: Drive and balance your truck
  • → Mouse: Tap through menus or select levels
  • → Mobile players can use touch buttons to control the truck.

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

  • → Start by selecting the first level on the locked map. Levels unlock as you progress.
  • → Your goal is to reach the finish line without losing all your health.
  • → Use the arrow keys to accelerate and balance your truck on rough terrain. Jump the car using the up arrow or W key.
  • → Zombies will be in your way; run them over for fun and coin rewards.
  • → Perform front or back flips during big jumps to earn 3 bonus coins for each one.
  • → If your truck lands upside down and stays flipped, your health bar drains fast. Don't panic; press the up arrow and maneuver to flip back quickly!
  • → Gather coins on the road. These coins let you unlock cooler and stronger trucks from the garage.
  • → Replay levels to collect more coins and master your flips.

Features

  • → Post-apocalyptic side-scrolling action
  • → Waves of zombies to crush under your tires
  • → 30+ levels
  • → 5 brutal trucks in garage
  • → Flip bonus mechanic
  • → Truck health bar system
