About This Game

Gulper.io - The Ultimate Snake Battle Royale

Dive into the deep with Gulper.io, a thrilling multiplayer snake game where you collect Tron-like light cycles to grow massive! Outmaneuver rivals, avoid deadly walls, and climb the leaderboard in this intense .io battleground.

Master the Depths: Gameplay Guide

Become a terrifying deep-sea gulper eel! Compete against hundreds of players in real-time as you:

  • 🐍 Collect light cycles to grow your serpentine body
  • 💥 Avoid collisions - one touch means starting over!
  • 🏆 Dominate the arena by becoming the largest predator

Customize Your Killer Eel

Personalize your gulper with vibrant colors and a unique nickname before diving into battle. Use the color slider to stand out in this underwater snake war!

Turbo Boost Tactics

Press SPACEBAR for a speed burst to:

  • ⚡ Dodge enemy snakes
  • 💨 Scoop up light cycles faster
  • 🏃‍♂️ Claim defeated players' pellets

Advanced Hunting Strategies

As you grow, trap opponents by circling them! Larger snakes can:

  • 🌀 Create inescapable coils
  • 🚧 Block escape routes
  • 👑 Dominate the shrinking play area

Private Battlegrounds

Create exclusive rooms with friends using the @roomname system, or jump into public matches instantly!

Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse: Steer your gulper
  • 🚀 Spacebar/Click: Speed boost
  • 📱 Touch: Mobile-friendly controls
