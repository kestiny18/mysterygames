About This Game

Geometry Vibes: X-Ball is a pulse-pounding platformer where you guide a neon ball through treacherous obstacle courses with just one button. Master the simple yet addictive tap-and-release mechanics to navigate deadly spikes, sharp slopes, and tight corridors in this modern tribute to classic Geometry Dash games.

Controls

  • Tap/Hold: Move upwards
  • Release: Flip direction

Game Modes

Classic Mode

10 progressively challenging levels with precision platforming and instant-death obstacles.

Race Mode

2-4 player local multiplayer chaos on shared keyboard - last ball standing wins!

Endless Mode

Test your endurance as speed increases indefinitely - how far can you go?

Challenge Mode

5 brutally difficult levels for expert players seeking ultimate bragging rights.

Features

  • 4 addictive game modes
  • 25+ challenging levels
  • Customizable ball skins
  • 4-player local multiplayer
  • 6 language support
