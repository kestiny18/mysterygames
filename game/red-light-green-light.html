About This Game

Red Light Green Light is a game from the famous TV series Squid Game, and it is definitely one of the most iconic scenes. In this children game, players stand behind a starting line and must reach a finish line at the far end of the field. A giant, creepy doll stands at the finish line. It turns its back to the players and says "Green light!" This means players can move. Then, when it suddenly says "Red light!" and turns around. And players must freeze immediately. If the doll sees someone still moving during "Red light," that player is eliminated (and in Squid Game, elimination is done by shooting the player).

In this online video game version of Red Light Green Light on OnlineGames.io, you'll be put in the tracksuit of a numbered contestant and dropped into a deadly playground. Movement can mean either survival or death. Nothing in between. So hit the play button and enjoy the same thrill 456 lived in the first episode!

Controls

  • PC:
    • Hold the Left Mouse Button or Spacebar to run
    • Release to stop when the doll turns
  • Mobile:
    • Tap and hold to move, release to stop

How to Play Red Light Green Light

  • Start by entering your name to join the round.
  • Line up with dozens of other players, all dressed in green tracksuits with their unique numbers.
  • When the doll looks away, run toward the red line by tapping and holding. The moment she turns back release the tap and freeze instantly.
  • If you get caught moving during "Red Light," and you're eliminated on the spot.
  • Make it to the finish line within the time limit to win and play the next round until you win the big prize.
  • If you want to stand out from the crowd, unlock different hats and headgears in the shop.

Tips & Tricks for Red Light, Green Light

  • Watch the doll's head closely; her body starts turning a second before she says, "Red Light."
  • Stay calm when the timer is close to running out. Panic leads to mistakes.
  • Don't try to sprint the whole way. Stop-start rhythm is your best strategy.
  • Save up your coins to unlock cool hats that make your player stand out!
