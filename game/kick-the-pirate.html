About This Game

Ahoy matey! Pirates may be brave on the high seas, but they've never met you. In Kick the Pirate, your job is simple: turn this grumpy pirate into your personal punching bag. He's muttering curses or just standing there looking smug. But it's oddly satisfying to send him flying with a cannonball. Seriously. Bad day at school? Boss annoyed you? This destroy game is the perfect way to blow off some steam without flipping a table.

Stress-Busting Clicker Game

Controls

  • Click or tap to attack the pirate.
  • Use earned coins to unlock more weapons.
  • Toggle settings in the top right for sound and blood effects.

How to Play Kick the Pirate

  • Start by clicking or tapping the pirate as fast (or as furiously) as you like.
  • Each hit earns you coins, which you'll use to buy new weapons.
  • Your goal is to deplete the pirate's health bar (at the bottom) to complete each level.
  • As you progress, the levels get tougher and the pirate gets sassier.
  • You can swap out the ship's background if you want a change of scene.
  • Toggle off blood splatter for less graphic visuals.

Weapons to Try

Unlock wacky weapons, each more satisfying than the last:

  • Flintlock Pistol: Classic and simple.
  • Bone Key Gun: Straight from a cursed treasure chest.
  • Battle Axe: Heavy damage and major pirate pain.
  • Ship Anchor: Watch him go flying.
  • Cannon: Boom! Need we say more?
  • TNT Barrel: Great for pirate control.
  • Rifle: Unlockable after brutal beating.

Developer

Developed by FreezeNova with casual fun in mind - perfect for quick sessions or all-out pirate punishment marathons.

Is Kick the Pirate Safe for Kids?

While using cartoon-style visuals (with optional blood effects), the gameplay revolves around exaggerated pirate violence. Recommended for players 10+ with parental guidance if needed.
