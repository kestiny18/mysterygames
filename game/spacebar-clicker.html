About This Game

Try not to wear out your spacebar in Spacebar Clicker! This addictive clicker game challenges you to smash the space key relentlessly to earn points. Spend them on hilarious boosters like Monkeys and Boomer Moms to automate your gains! 🚀

About Spacebar Clicker

A giant cream-colored spacebar dominates your screen - your mission? Click it into oblivion! Each tap earns +1 point, which unlocks game-changing boosters. Pro tip: double your output by using both on-screen and physical spacebars simultaneously!

Meet Your Booster Squad

  • Monkey: Keyboard vandal! Randomly mashes buttons every 5 seconds
  • Boomer Mom: Technophobic but determined - earns 3 points/sec
  • Gen Z Kid: Scrolls instead of types, but racks up 20 points/sec
  • Keyboard Upgrade: Doubles all your click earnings!

Pro Clicking Strategies

Before boosters save you, try multitapping techniques: combine spacebar hits with mouse clicks for maximum velocity. Your keyboard may never recover!

Simple Controls

Desktop:

  • SPACE key or mouse click = Points!

Mobile:

  • Tap the virtual spacebar
