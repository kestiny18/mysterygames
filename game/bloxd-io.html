About This Game

Welcome to Bloxd io, an ultimate online Minecraft game with diverse modes. Whether you're interested in peaceful building or intense PvP, Bloxd io delivers!

Choose from survival, building, battling, or mystery-solving modes. Play solo or with friends online.

Controls

  • Move Forward: W
  • Move Backward: S
  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Run: Shift
  • Break Block: Left Click
  • Place Block: Right Click / E
  • Open Inventory: Tab
  • Drop Item: Q
  • Jump: Space
  • Crouch: Control / C
  • Pick Block: Middle Mouse
  • Zoom: V

Game Modes

Discover 6 exciting categories of gameplay:

Open World

  • Survival: PvP combat in an open world - collect resources and outlast others
  • Peaceful: Creative building without PvP dangers
  • Creative: Unlimited blocks and fly mode for ultimate building

PvP Team

  • Bed Wars: Protect your team's bed while attacking others'
  • Pirates: Naval combat destroying enemy ships
  • Hide and Seek: Transform into blocks and evade seekers

Building

  • One Block: Start with one block and expand your world
  • Doodle Cube: Theme-based building competitions
  • Plots: Safe private building areas

Free For All

  • Sky Wars: Battle royale on floating islands
  • Infection: Zombie survival horror

Special

  • Murder Mystery: Detective vs murderer gameplay
  • Worlds: Create custom game experiences
