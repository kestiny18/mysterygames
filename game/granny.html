About This Game

Granny is a horror-puzzle game where your survival skills, quick thinking, and stealth will be working non-stop. This scary game takes place in a Granny's eerie house, where you wake up in a bloody bed and realize you're trapped. Your room's door is locked from the outside, and your ultimate goal is to escape from this hell. But it's not that simple. Granny has a surprisingly sharp hearing for her age. She is always on the hunt and has a knack for finding you. This game is a mix of fear, strategy, and problem-solving that will keep your heart racing and your mind working overtime. So, if you're ready for jumpscare moments, wear your headphones, hit the play button and see if you can escape from Granny's House!

⚠ Mature Content Warning
This horror game features intense fear-based elements, graphic imagery, and disturbing themes. It is intended for mature audiences and may not be suitable for all players. If you're sensitive to frightening content, please explore our other free games. Player discretion is advised.

Controls

For Computers:

  • WASD: Move
  • Mouse: Look around
  • E: Interact
  • C: Sit/Stand
  • R: Hide
  • Space: Throw objects
  • F: Escape traps

For Mobiles:

  • Joystick (Left): Move
  • Touchpad (Right): Look around
  • Hand Icon: Interact
  • Chair Icon: Sit/Stand
  • Bed Arrow Icon: Hide
  • Item Drop Icon: Throw objects

How to Play Granny

  • If you're new to the game or just want to explore without the heart-pounding chase, try Practice mode. Here, Granny is away, giving you a chance to plan your escape.
  • For those seeking a challenge, there are five difficulty levels to choose from, ranging from easy to extreme. Pick the level and hit start.
  • You have five attempts to succeed. Granny hears every sound, so tread carefully and avoid noisy mistakes like knocking over objects or stepping on creaky floors.
  • The house is filled with mysterious items like keys, closets, books, and tools. Press E to interact with them.
  • Each item has a purpose, but you'll need to figure out how to use them to unlock doors or access hidden areas. Explore the house, find clues, and solve puzzles.
  • Remember that there is no single way of escaping. In order to make a great getaway, you can try one of the multiple escape routes available. You can aim to unlock the front door or try fixing up the old car in the garage... It's up to you.

Tips for a Smooth Getaway

  • Avoid making noise at all costs—Granny reacts to even the faintest sounds.
  • Beds, closets, and chests make great hiding spots if Granny is nearby.
  • Be mindful of where you drop items, as they can create noise and reveal your location.
  • Watch your step! Granny's traps can slow you down or leave you vulnerable.
  • Use weapons sparingly—they can hinder Granny, but only for a brief time.
  • You can gain five additional attempts to escape by watching ads.
