About This Game

Paper-airplanes and daydreams, but this time louder. Crazy Plane Landing is a 3D stunt flight game where you launch a plane off a ramp, try to keep it in one piece and land on the runway. Every barrel roll and extra meter adds to your payout. Spend coins on upgrades then chase that distant finish line across different terrains. Play free in your browser on OnlineGames.io - no installs, just click and fly.

Controls

  • Left Click & Hold: Launch plane and burn boost
  • A/D Keys: Steer mid-air and perform barrel rolls

Launch and Fly

  • Time your boost - too early stalls, too late overshoots
  • Barrel rolls earn coins but risk crashes
  • Progress through canyons, peaks, cliffs and neon cities

Upgrade Planes

  • Earn coins based on distance, stunts and landing
  • Upgrade Engine (speed), Boost (airtime) and Bonus (coin multiplier)
  • Unlock new planes to smash records

Developed by BoomBit S.A. - also creators of Super Hero Driving School. Explore more plane games after mastering Crazy Plane Landing!
