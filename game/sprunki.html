About This Game

Sprunki is a fun and creative music game where you can become a DJ. Dress up characters from a quirky band called the Sprunki by dragging and dropping cool clothes and accessories onto them. Each costume or accessory you choose adds a different musical sound, such as bass, melody, or drums. Enter the world of colorful Sprunkis, like Mr. Tree, Mr Sun, Lime and Garnold. If you're feeling extra whimsical, put the black hat on any member. And things will take a spooky turn. The theme gets darker, and visuals and sounds get scarier. Each hat and each accessory you put on makes the Sprunkis feel like they ran away from a horror movie.

Controls

  • Computers: Use your mouse to drag and drop accessories
  • Mobiles: Drag and drop accessories by tapping

How to Play Sprunki

  • Begin by picking a fun character from the lineup, each with its own special sound.
  • You'll see different outfits or accessories at the bottom.
  • Dress each of the Sprunks to give them unique sound elements.
  • You'll encounter quirky items like devil horns, tree and sun costumes, bunny ears and even satellite dishes along the way. You cannot use an item on multiple Sprunks. If you'd like to reset the band, click on the replay button on the top right.
  • Mix and match different music loops and sounds to create beautiful melodies or experimental tracks.
  • If you want to concentrate on one character's sound, just hover over them and click the headphone icon to mute the others.
  • Similarly, you can mute one's sound by hovering over them and clicking the mute icon.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different outfits to discover hidden musical surprises!

What Is Sprunki Incredibox?

Incredibox is a unique experimental music app that lets you conduct a group of beatboxers. Sprunki Incredibox originated as a fun, fan-made mode created by a 15-year-old Incredibox user named NyankoBfLol. It skyrocketed and quickly gained popularity, attracting an audience twice the size of the original Incredibox. What makes Sprunki truly unique is its shift from a joyful music game to a more eerie experience. The bright colors slowly change to darker shades, and your characters gain spooky looks. This creepy twist of this mode captured the imagination of users, leading to its own standalone game.

Even more surprisingly, Sprunki characters have emerged as new horror mascots, joining the ranks of figures like the Rainbow Friends, Freddy Fazbear, Granny, and Poppy. Fans all around the world have contributed to the Sprunki hype in a way. Today, you can find Sprunks not only in online music-making games but also across various genres, such as horror games, platformers, trivia games, and more. For example, in Obby: Find Sprunki game, you can go for a Sprunki hunt and find hidden characters on an obstacle course. There are even fan fiction stories that explore themes around Sprunkis being orphans or having disabilities, adding depth to their intriguing lore.
