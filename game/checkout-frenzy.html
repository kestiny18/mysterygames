About This Game

Ever wondered what cashiers do on those screens? All those beeps and buttons... Checkout Frenzy turns that mystery into an addictive online game where you run the show!

Build your mini-mart empire from scratch - stock shelves, checkout customers, and hire staff in this chaotic cashier simulator. Experience the thrill of retail without leaving your browser!

Gameplay Highlights

  • WASD: Navigate your store
  • Left Click: Scan items, arrange shelves, manage payments
  • Drag: Rotate furniture for optimal store layout

Pro Tips

Price items smartly - too high and customers leave, too low and profits suffer. Hire cashiers when busy, expand your space when profitable!
