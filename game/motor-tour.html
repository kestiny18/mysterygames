Controls

  • W: Move Forward
  • A: Turn Left
  • D: Turn Right
  • S: Brake
  • Left Shift: Nitro Boost
  • ESC: Go Back/Pause

How to Play Motor Tour

You can start by selecting your favorite game mode: Career, Special Event, Endless, or Race. For beginners, it's a good idea to practice in endless mode first. You'll cruise down a busy highway until you crash, so it's perfect to get a feel for the bike's handling.

Or you can try career mode where you'll tackle different missions under time pressure. It starts with relatively easy missions and the difficulty ramps up slowly. You can prove you can handle tight turns and avoid reckless drivers while collecting enough cash to unlock more upgrades. In Race mode, challenge tough AI opponents in an intense head-to-head showdown, collect nitrous on the road to gain an edge over your rival. Want something different? Special Event mode offers fun tasks and big loot rewards.

Game Features

  • 40 Motorbikes: With 40 different bikes in the garage, you won't run out of choices. Each one has its own style, and you can show your personality by customizing your license plate. Represent your country with its flag, and write a cool message on the plate.
  • Deep Upgrades: Boost power, turbo, brakes, acceleration, handling, and nitro. Each upgrade helps you handle your bike better and improve overall performance. The best way to dominate the road is to keep enhancing those stats.
  • Multiple Game Modes: Pick from four main modes: Career, Special Event, Endless, or Race. Each one offers a new challenge, whether you love timed missions, intense races, or endless cruising.
  • Premium 3D Graphics: The game sports impressive visuals that make every ride feel realistic. From the shine on your motorbike to the detailed cityscape, you'll be drawn into the action.
  • Online Rewards: Motor Tour lets you watch ads to earn quick cash, gold, or blueprints. It might be worth it if you're in a hurry to get that new upgrade or unlock a new bike!
