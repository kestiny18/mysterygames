About This Game

Say hi to an easily controlled and captivating game! Drift Boss is a 3D driving game where you steer your little car over sharp bends. No brakes are holding you. Drift through the corners and swing into high gear. The aim is to drive as long as possible without falling into space. Drift Boss makes such a deep addiction that you will be surprised how simple mechanics make this possible. Don't forget to share your highest score with us in the comment section below!

How to Play Drift Boss?

  • Hit the start button and activate a booster if you have one.
  • Press the spacebar (or left click) to turn right and release the control to turn left.
  • The path you drive on has no ends, so you set your own goals.
  • Beat your highest score, or challenge your friends and be the boss of drifting!

Drift Like a Boss: Boosters

Three kinds of boosters are available in Drift Boss: Double score, car insurance, and coin rush. You can activate the boosters for only one run, and they will make your run easier for a while. You need to set the booster before driving.

  • Car Insurance: This booster will insure your car for one fall in a run. If you fall into space, you can continue where you left off by using your insurance.
  • Double Score: This will double your score through the run.
  • Coin Rush: This booster will fill your roads with coins. A great option if you are planning to buy a new vehicle.

How do I earn boosters in Drift Boss?

  • you can buy them with your coins💰
  • you can turn the spin wheel and cross your fingers for the best 🎰
  • check in every day to take daily prizes🎁

Need Some Tips?

Here are the tips and tricks for Drift Boss:

  • Don't steer too early. Wait until when you are close to the bend, then make your turn.
  • Are you frustrated falling down over and over? Try to buy a new vehicle; more expensive cars have better grip. Then, you will enjoy a longer ride without thinking about the sliding distance.
  • You can turn your car on the air if you come across a bend right after jumping.
  • Remember to get your daily rewards and practice. You will soon be an Ultimate Drift Boss!

Features

  • Easy controls and simple mechanics
  • Daily rewards and spin wins
  • Different boosters available
  • Range of vehicles to unlock
  • Mobile compatible

Controls

  • Desktop: Press space or left click to turn right, release to steer left
  • Mobile: Touch and hold the screen to turn right, and release it to turn left
