About This Game

Drift King stands out among online drift games with its unique rearview smoke effects and premium 3D visuals. Choose from 10 sports cars and 6 detailed maps, with comprehensive tuning options for the ultimate drifting experience.

Controls

  • WASD/arrows: Move car
  • M: Rearview Mirror
  • C: Change Camera
  • R: Reset Car
  • U: Toggle KMH/MPH
  • I: Start/Stop Engine
  • P: Fullscreen

How To Play

Start with 30,000 coins to buy and tune cars immediately. Explore the garage, upgrade parts, then choose from 6 diverse maps for your perfect drift experience.

Garage Features

  • • Upgrade engine, turbo, handling
  • • Custom paint jobs
  • • Advanced suspension tuning
  • • Premium wheel selection

Multiplayer

Create rooms with friends for competitive drifting, races, or chaotic chases in real-time multiplayer mode.
