About This Game

GT Cars City Racing is an exhilarating 3D driving adventure featuring split-screen multiplayer, GT supercars, and explosive sandbox mayhem! Race through Toy City's physics playground with wild mini-games: demolish robots, launch into pools, collect diamonds, and topple buildings. Compete in Face to Face races where your car changes every lap, or survive the Mega Ramps time trial against a destructive robot. Fully customize your ride with paint jobs, neon effects, and rim colors.

Controls

Player 1

  • Move: WASD
  • Nitro: Shift
  • Repair: R
  • Camera: C

Player 2

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: N
  • Repair: U
  • Camera: K
