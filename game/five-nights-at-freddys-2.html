About This Game

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 (FNaF 2) is a popular horror game with a spooky environment and thrilling narrative. If you have the courage, play it now for free on OnlineGames.io!

Controls

Desktops

  • Interact with lights: Mouse
  • Turn on flashlight: Hold Space
  • Wear a mask and access cameras: Hover the Mouse Over Two Panels

What is the story of Five Nights at Freddy's?

Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria opened with wild enthusiasm to serve pizzas and entertain children with animatronics, but was followed by a series of tragic events. Reports of missing children surfaced, along with rumors of murders and haunted animatronics that allegedly bit children to death.

In Five Nights at Freddy's 2, the pizzeria reopens and you take the night security guard position at this haunted restaurant. "What could go wrong?" you think as you begin your shift from 12 AM to 6 AM.

How to Play FNAF 2

  • Survive five consecutive nights by monitoring security cameras to track haunted animatronics that move when you're not watching
  • Manage your limited flashlight battery and prepare for terrifying jump scares

Stay Alive

The animatronics will attack if you're not careful - keep watching them through the cameras and survive until 6 AM each night!

Get Ready for Jumpscares!

Play this terrifying horror game at your own risk - those animatronics could appear anytime!
