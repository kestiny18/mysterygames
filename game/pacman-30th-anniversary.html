About This Game

Who's not afraid of ghosts? Pac-Man is!

In the neon glow of 1980s arcades, a legendary yellow hero was born - shaped like a pizza missing a slice! While ghosts may scare him at first, power pellets turn the tables in this timeless chase. Creator Toru Iwatani crafted Pac-Man's relationship with the ghosts to mirror the classic Tom & Jerry dynamic.

Celebrating 30 years of wakka-wakka magic, Google immortalized Pac-Man as an interactive Doodle - complete with the Google logo cleverly hidden in the maze! This special edition features enhanced graphics and browser accessibility, bringing retro arcade thrills to modern players.

Ready to master the maze? Our ultimate guide unlocks everything: gameplay secrets, fascinating trivia, world records, and answers to burning questions about both the classic and Google Doodle versions of this iconic game.

How to Play Pac-Man

  • Guide the yellow hero through mazes to collect all dots while avoiding colorful ghosts
  • Power pellets turn ghosts blue - chase and eat them for bonus points!
  • Collect fruits for extra points as difficulty increases each level
  • Master ghost patterns to achieve high scores and break records

Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Move Pac-Man
  • WASD: Control Ms. Pac-Man (2-player mode)
  • Click: Insert Coin

What's the Google Doodle version?

What's special about Level 256?

The infamous "kill screen" caused by technical limitations creates a glitched, nearly impossible challenge.
