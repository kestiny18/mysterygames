About This Game

Hop on your bike and start riding in the wild world of Obby But You're on a Bike! This fun 3D platformer game allows you to tackle crazy obstacle courses as your Roblox character rides through ramps, loops, and narrow parkour. Each of the 8 worlds is packed with hundreds of flags to collect, making every ride an adventure. But there's a twist—you have only 8 lives per level. Fall off the platform, and you'll lose a life. Use them all, and you'll have to restart that level from the beginning. Upgrade your bike, unlock boosters, and prove you're the ultimate Obby master. Don't wait—start your riding journey on OnlineGames.io today! 🚴✨

Controls

Computer:

  • Use WASD keys to move.
  • Press and hold the right mouse button, then move the mouse to look around.
  • Press the Spacebar to jump.

Mobile:

  • Move with the joystick on the left side of the screen.
  • Drag across the screen to look around.
  • Tap the button on the right side of the joystick to jump.

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

  • Hop on your bike and head into world 1 by entering that green door.
  • Ride through ramps, loops, and tricky platforms to reach the finish line.
  • Each level gives you 8 lives. Falling off the platform costs one life, and losing them all means starting the level over.
  • Collect as many flags as you can to boost your score.
  • Upgrade your bike to improve speed and handling, and collect boosters to ride faster because, let's face it, your character's legs aren't getting any stronger.🚴
  • Complete all 8 levels to claim victory and brag about your Obby skills!

Game Features

  • Unlock better bikes to improve your chances of success.
  • Use powerful boosts to clear tough jumps and obstacles.
  • Unlock different skins like Barry, policeman and more.
  • Spin wheels to grab prizes or complete missions to claim your award.
