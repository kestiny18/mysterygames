Julie Beauty Salon

Surprise Me!

About This Game

Julie Beauty Salon is a glam up makeover game where you run a beauty salon. Every makeover is a mini masterpiece in this fun, stylish browser game. You're a beauty expert in charge of transforming a fabulous girl with bold makeup, trendy hairstyles, and colorful manicures. If you've ever dreamed of being a stylist or just love mixing and matching beauty looks, this free game is perfect for you.

Controls

  • Click or tap to select makeup tools, hairstyles, and nail polish.

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

  • Complete unique beauty routines one by one: makeup, hair styling, and nail art for three different girls.
  • Start by selecting the first part from the intro screen. Our girl needs a full transformation!

Start with Makeup

  • Pick a lipstick, swipe on colorful blush, and choose stylish eyelashes from the right panel. Want soft glam or bold drama? You decide!
  • If you complete the makeup, move on to the next level: hair makeover!
  • Give your client a fresh new hairstyle. You can switch cuts, change colors (even go two-tone like pink and blue), and make them shine!
  • If she looks happy with the end result of her hair, next is NAILS!

Manicure & Nail Art

  • Make the manicure, and apply nail polish. Then add some glitter, french tips of your preferred color or some other nail art options.
  • Finish it off with rings, bracelets or watches to complete the look.
  • Once you've styled all three parts, you'll see green checkmarks on each portrait, meaning your job is done! Take a screenshot of your makeover and share it with friends! If you want, you can always press the refresh button to go wild with new looks.

Developer

Julie Beauty Salon is created by FreezeNova Games.

Is Julie Beauty Salon safe for kids?

Yes! Julie Beauty Salon is a kid-friendly makeover game with no violence or inappropriate content. It's perfect game for kids who enjoy fashion and creativity.
