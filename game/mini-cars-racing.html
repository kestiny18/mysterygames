About This Game

Mini Cars Racing is a 2 player car racing game you can play free on OnlineGames.io! Race against the clock or battle a friend on the same device. Fast, fun, and nostalgic like playing with toy cars.

Choose between Solo (vs CPU) or 2 Player split-screen local multiplayer on one keyboard. Tight controls, playful visuals, and wild twisty tracks await!

Race Modes:

  • Arcade Mode: 3-lap race against opponents
  • Time Trial: Beat your best solo time

Controls:

  • Player 1: W/S/A/D/C
  • Player 2: Arrow Keys + Keypad 1

Features:

  • 8 unique mini cars to choose
  • 3 challenging tracks with jumps and obstacles
  • Easy/Medium/Expert difficulty
  • Instant respawn when stuck

Developed by FreezeNova, creators of fun 2 player browser games. Try Car Football next for more vehicular mayhem!
