About This Game

Get ready to flip, jump, and dunk your way through wild levels in Hoop World 3D! This 3D basketball game isn't your average dunk contest - it's a high-flying physics adventure with trampolines, wormholes, and crazy stunts. Bounce across floating platforms, fly through space portals, and land perfect dunks while pulling off insane flips for bonus coins!

Each level presents new challenges - from simple jumps to inter-dimensional launches. Master the timing or enjoy hilarious ragdoll fails! Love basketball? Try Basketball io for more action!

Controls

Computer: Hold left-click/space to flip → Release to dunk

Mobile: Tap/hold to flip → Release to dunk

Gameplay Tips

  • Hold to flip → More spins = More coins
  • Aim for wormholes to unlock epic jumps
  • Time your release for perfect dunks
  • Unlock special characters like Hulk/Spiderman
