Gold Miner

Surprise Me!

About This Game

Gold Miner is a legendary flash game where you drop your claw, grab gold, and haul it up before time runs out. It's like fishing with a rod, except you're catching shiny rocks instead of fish. Each level gives you a money goal - collect treasures like gold and diamonds while avoiding worthless rocks. Make smart buys in the shop between rounds. One wrong drop and your claw wastes precious time on a boulder. Yet you'll keep coming back for more - this browser game has kept its charm for decades!

Controls

  • Drop Claw: Press ↓ when the swinging claw is lined up
  • Toss Dynamite: Press ↑ to discard unwanted items

How to Play

Time your claw drops to grab valuables - big items take longer but small gold is quicker. Each level has a timer and money goal. Accidentally hooked a rock? Use limited dynamite (costs money) to blow it up!

Shop Items

  • Dynamite ($51): Discard worthless items when seconds count
  • Lucky Clover ($14): Boosts mystery bag rewards for one level
  • Diamond Polish ($286): Increases diamond value next level

History

Created by GameRival in 2003, this Flash classic has been revived for modern browsers. Play free on OnlineGames.io and explore other nostalgic Flash games like Duck Life and Henry Stickmin.
