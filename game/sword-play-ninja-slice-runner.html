About This Game

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. You'll be slicing flying shurikens, bullets, shields or dodging massive bullets, just with your fingers moving. The game throws you into a simple but colorful 3D world that changes in each level. Complete sword fights, slicing action, and enjoy satisfying slow-motion effects. It's a mix of Fruit Ninja's slicing satisfaction and the fun of endless runners.

Controls

  • Swipe on the screen to slash enemies and obstacles.
  • Hold and drag to control your sword's direction.
  • Timing and precision are everything!

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner Online

  • Start running down a path filled with enemies.
  • As you move, swipe to slice through foes who rush at you with weapons.
  • Time your moves to dodge traps like swinging axes or bullets.
  • Finish each level by defeating the final enemy or completing a stylish slow-motion slash.
  • Shielded enemies who need multiple hits.
  • Special weapons like dual katanas or energy blades.
  • Satisfying cinematic slow-mo kills that make you feel like a true ninja.

Customize Your Blade and Style Your Swings

One of the coolest parts of Sword Play! Ninja Slice Runner is the wide range of sword skins and trail effects you can unlock in the shop. They make your slicing feel even more fun and rewarding.

Sword Skins

You can unlock random swords with 700 coins or collect specific ones by leveling up or completing challenges:

  • A jagged flame-shaped sword (your default)
  • A giant black cleaver
  • A sci-fi energy blade (pixel-style!)
  • A chainsaw sword for intense slicing
  • A key-shaped sword (unlock it with keys from gameplay)
  • Even a hockey stick or a curved fantasy bow at higher levels

Sword Trails

Leave a mark, literally. These visual effects make every attack more exciting:

  • Smooth white arcs or star-studded slashes
  • Bubble trails or lightning-style swings
  • Colorful effects like glowing pink trails (unlocked at level 10)
  • Fiery orange swings (unlocked at level 20)

Tip: Trails make every hit more stylish and VR-like experience!
