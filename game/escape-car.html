About This Game

Do you remember the very first Grand Theft Auto? That old-school, top-down chaos. Think of that and give it a fresh coat of chunky 3D graphics. Next, drop that straight in your browser. Yes, it's Escape Car, a free online GTA game where you bolt through a mini-city, dodge police cars, tanks, helicopters and scoop up stacks of cash. Every tight drift, every split-second fake-out, and every totaled police cruiser bumps your score higher. Hang on long enough, and you'll leave with pockets full of upgrade money and a grin wider than the avenue.

Controls

  • A or Left Arrow to steer left
  • D or Right Arrow to steer right.

How to Play Escape Car

  • The moment you touch A or D, the police chase begins.
  • Drive as long as you possibly can without letting the cops box you in. Grab the cash piles on the road for extra money.
  • The BUSTED bar at the top fills with every bump; solid red means game over.
  • The longer you survive, the faster the score counter on the top left ticks.
  • Watch the stars next to your score; each new star means tougher cops and bigger points. First more cars show up, then heavy trucks, and later even tanks and helicopters.
  • When you finally bite the curb, your score instantly converts to money for upgrades. Unlock a new faster car from the garage and repeat. Hit restart and see if that next run tops your record.

Unlock Cool Cars

Money burning a hole in your pocket? Slide over to the Garage and spend it on a random mystery car. You might pull:

  • a tuner that slides around corners like butter,
  • a muscle car that turns cruisers into scrap, or
  • a sports coupe perfect for weaving through traffic.

Collect them all and build the dream garage you brag about in the comments. No two runs feel the same once you start swapping rides.
