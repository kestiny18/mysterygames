About This Game

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note. It's a free online tank game that looks like it was drawn during a very boring math class cuz this game is so FUN! Every line, laser fence, and armored turret is sketched on squared notebook paper. The action feels as intense as any full-blown arcade shooter. You'll guide a tiny hand-colored tank through mazes of spinning barriers, pencil-red laser grids, and enemy doodles that won't hesitate to erase you from the page. Plus, you can play against a friend on the same keyboard. This free game is wrapped in fun in a charming paper-craft style that makes the battlefield feel both nostalgic and brand-new.

Controls

Purple Tank (Player 1)

  • Move W / A / S / D
  • Shoot Space
  • Restart R

Green Tank (Player 2)

  • Move Arrow Keys
  • Shoot M
  • Restart R

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Choose between 1 P and 2 P on the title page. In single-player, you face an obstacle course broken into three main zones, each gated by glowing red laser doors. Destroy marked targets, tanks, turrets, or clusters of foot soldiers to shut those lasers down and unlock the next area. Your green health bar at the top left shows how much scribbled armor you have left. When it empties, your tank gets crunched into paper scraps and the run ends.

Solo Campaign

Each zone contains five high-value targets. Pick them off in any order while weaving through cross-shaped barriers and timing your moves past rotating cannons. Projectiles travel only in the direction your tank is facing, so line up your turret before shooting. Some walls are thin enough to fire through; others bounce shots back, turning the page into a ricochet puzzle. Clear all fifteen targets across the three zones to complete the mission and claim the ruler-of-the-paper-note title!

Grab Power-Ups

Colored icons dot the battlefield, each granting an instant upgrade when you roll over it. In two-player mode, these bonuses spawn more often, so snag them fast or watch your friend grab the upper hand.

  • Armor adds a chunk to your health bar, letting you soak up extra damage.
  • Repair fully restores your tank
  • Bigger Bullet boosts shell size for wider impact.
  • Triple Shots fires a spread of three doodle rounds at once.
  • Rocket swaps your standard shell for a fast, splash-damage missile.
  • Laser unleashes a piercing beam that carves clean lines through enemies.
  • Freeze temporarily locks every hostile unit in place
  • Laser Inactivation turns off certain red grids
