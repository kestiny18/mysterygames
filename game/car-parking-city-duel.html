About This Game

Car Parking City Duel is a free online driving game where you can play solo or on split-screen with a friend. It features a 3D open-world city with realistic driving physics and multiple game modes: racing, drifting, crashing, timed missions, and free exploration. This car game combines various driving challenges into one exciting package.

Controls

Player 1

  • Move: WASD
  • Switch Camera: C
  • NOS: Left Shift
  • Brake: Space
  • Restart: R
  • Look Back: T

Player 2

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Switch Camera: K
  • NOS: O
  • Brake: P
  • Restart: U
  • Look Back: L

Game Modes

Race Mode

Compete against friends on 10 unique tracks with tight corners and city traffic. Perfect your NOS boosts to win!

Missions

20 challenging parking scenarios requiring precision and timing. Watch your health bar!

Crash Mode

Earn points by smashing cars - the more destruction, the higher your score!

Drift Mode

Maintain high-speed drifts around corners to rack up points and show your skills.
