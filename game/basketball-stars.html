About This Game

Basketball Stars is a free online basketball game that you can enjoy right in your browser. Start strong, showcase your three-point skills, dominate rebounds, and leave opponents no breathing room! Become a legend on the court - play solo or with friends in thrilling tournaments or fast-paced matches!

How to Play

Player 1 Controls:

  • WASD: Move
  • X: Shoot/Steal
  • S: Pump/Block
  • Double A/D: Dash
  • K: Super Shot

Player 2 Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Move
  • L: Shoot/Steal
  • Down Arrow: Pump/Block
  • Double Left/Right: Dash
  • Z: Super Shot

Game Features

  • 4 Exciting Modes: Tournament, Random Match, Training & Quick Match
  • Flexible Play: 1v1 or 2v2 with friends or against CPU
  • Authentic Teams: Featuring the best NBA teams and players

Dunk Like a Pro

Run to the hoop, jump (W/Up Arrow), then shoot (X/L) mid-air for that epic dunk!
