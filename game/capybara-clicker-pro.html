About This Game

Capybara Clicker Pro is an addictive idle clicker where you tap adorable capybaras to collect coins! Upgrade your earnings with boosters and unlock charming new characters as you progress.

How to Play

Simply tap the capybara to earn coins. Purchase boosters to increase your earnings and unlock new characters via the t-shirt icon.

Powerful Boosters

  • +100 coins/click with Professional Clicker
  • +200 coins/sec with Robot
  • Glitch Clicker gives +1000 coins/click
  • The Factory adds +5000 coins/sec

Adorable Characters

  • Capycorn
  • Kapybaru
  • Capybored
  • Camobara
  • Cosmic Capybara

Dynamic Weather

Change weather conditions (rain, snow, stars) via the cloud icon for visual variety!

Bonus Mechanic

Fill the pink bar for 2x coin bonuses during rapid tapping!

Simple Controls

  • Just tap the capybara to earn coins
