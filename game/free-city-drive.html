About This Game

Free City Drive is an immersive car simulator where you conquer open roads at breakneck speeds! Choose your dream car and customize every detail: Color, License Plate, Spoiler. Thrilling Game Modes: Free Drive, 30 Challenging Missions with epic rewards, Test Area for ultimate fun. Controls: WASD to drive, Shift for nitro boost, Space handbrake, Tab/Esc pause, C switch cameras.
