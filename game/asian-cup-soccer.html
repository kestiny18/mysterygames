About This Game

Score the winning goal under stadium lights! Asian Cup Soccer delivers fast-paced football action with simple one-button controls. Choose from Asia's elite teams like Japan, South Korea, or Australia and battle through knockout rounds where every shot could be decisive. No complex rules - just aim, shoot, and defend your way to glory!

⚽ Game Controls

Shooting:

  • Click 1: Aim direction
  • Click 2: Set shot height
  • Click 3: Add curve (advanced)

Goalkeeping:

  • Quickly tap the gloves when they appear to make saves!

🏆 Tournament Mode

Select from 12 national teams and compete through the Round of 16 to reach the championship final. Each match is a high-pressure showdown!

🎯 Shooting Mechanics

Control your shots with three simple clicks: set direction, adjust height, and add curve to outsmart defenders. It's like basketball meets paper football on a real pitch!

🧤 Goalkeeping Challenge

When defending, react quickly to glove indicators showing shot trajectory. Lightning-fast reflexes earn you clean sheets!

🏅 Achievements

Earn prestigious trophies including:

  • ★ First Victory
  • ★ Finalist
  • ★ Champion
  • ⚽ Golden Boot (20+ goals)
