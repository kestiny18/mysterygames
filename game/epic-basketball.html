About This Game

Epic Basketball is a fun and quirky street-style sports game you can play right in your browser. It's all about how many baskets you can sink in just 60 seconds. If a basketball shootout game sounds too easy, think again because this hoop doesn't like to stay in one spot, and distractions are everywhere. Stay sharp, aim true, and shoot your shot!

Controls

  • Desktop: Hold down the left mouse button, drag to aim, and release to shoot.
  • Mobile: Tap and drag on the screen to set your angle release to launch the ball.

How to Play Epic Basketball

  • You start by picking your player. At first, you'll play as Javi, a funky street baller with big hair and big dreams.
  • Once you're on the court, you'll face a single hoop. You have 60 seconds to begin with. Your goal is to score as many points as possible before time runs out.
  • Scoring isn't the only challenge. Each time you make a basket, the hoop moves. Sometimes farther, sometimes higher. You'll have to adjust your aim and force. The game uses a dotted arc to help you plan your shot, but mastering the rhythm takes practice.
  • A small extra hoop sometimes appears on the screen. If you score while it's active, you get bonus time!

Unlock Epic Basketball Players

Start with Javi and unlock more colorful players like Plin Plin, Bubu, Patty Wally, Bruce, Alien, Neil, Imhotep and more. Each character has their own personality and look, but the real prize is Nona. She may look like a grandma, but she's the ultimate baller. To unlock Nona, you'll need to score 300 points. That's not easy, so get practicing!
